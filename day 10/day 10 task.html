11:What is href vs src attributes?

HREF stands for HyperText Reference, while SRC stands for Source Reference. HREF is used to refer to another HTML element, whereas SRC refers to a non-HTML file. In other words, HREF is used to refer to pages on the same website, whereas SRC refers to pages on different servers.
